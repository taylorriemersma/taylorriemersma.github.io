About Taylor Riemersma

This May I’ll graduate from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln with a Bachelor of Journalism degree in Sports Media and Communication. I currently work in the Department of Nebraska Athletics Communications Department and as a tutor. Soon, I will take on the position of being an official for intramural flag football as well here at UNL.

My R Studio skills are still developing as I am in my second coding class, but I’m always eager to learn more and improve my abilities.

I have a strong passion for collegiate baseball and hope to work with a collegiate baseball team in the future. I’m open to a lot of opportunities that may come my way and keep the door open the best I can each step of the way.
